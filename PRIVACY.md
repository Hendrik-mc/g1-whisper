# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 2025

## What data we collect

- **Microphone audio**: Only when you explicitly start a voice widget. Audio is streamed directly to OpenAI's Realtime API and is not stored on device or by this app.
- **Bluetooth scan results**: Used only to find and connect to your G1 glasses. Not stored or transmitted.
- **No analytics**: The app contains no analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.

## What we do NOT collect

- Location data
- Contacts or calendar data
- Device identifiers or advertising IDs
- Crash reports sent to third parties

## Data controls

- You can pause the microphone at any time from the dashboard
- You can disconnect your glasses at any time
- Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored preferences

## Third-party services

- **OpenAI Realtime API**: Audio is sent to OpenAI when a voice widget is active. See [OpenAI Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/privacy).

## Contact

For privacy questions, open an issue on the GitHub repository.
